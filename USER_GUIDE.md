# Equipment Store App User Guide

## Introduction
The Equipment Store app helps you manage equipment, consumables, calibration, maintenance, reservations, and audit logs. This guide explains how to use the app as an end user.

---

## Getting Started
1. **Login:**
   - Enter your credentials to access the app.
   - If you do not have an account, contact your administrator.

2. **Dashboard:**
   - Overview of equipment status, recent activity, and quick actions.

---

## Equipment Management
### Check Out Equipment
- Go to the "Check Out" page.
- Select equipment from the list.
- Choose destination (including calibration, site-to-site transfer, etc.).
- Set condition (Excellent, Good, Poor).
- If not Excellent or condition worsens, provide a reason.
- Confirm checkout. A confirmation dialog will appear.
- The equipment is now checked out and the action is logged.

### Check In Equipment
- Go to the "Check In" page.
- Select equipment to check in.
- Update condition and add notes if needed.
- Confirm check-in. The action is logged.

---

## Consumables
- Go to the "Consumables" page.
- Add new consumables by entering details.
- Duplicate checks prevent adding the same item twice.
- View and manage consumable inventory.

---

## Calibration
- Go to the "Calibration" page.
- Add calibration records for equipment.
- Select equipment, enter calibration details, and save.
- Calibration actions are logged for audit.

---

## Maintenance
- Go to the "Maintenance" page.
- Add maintenance logs for equipment.
- Enter maintenance details and save.
- View maintenance history for each item.

---

## Reservations
- Go to the "Reservations" page.
- Reserve equipment for future use.
- Select equipment, set reservation dates, and confirm.
- View and manage reservations.

---

## Audit Log
- Go to the "Audit Log" page.
- View all actions performed in the app.
- Filter by table, action, user, or date.
- Click on entries for detailed information.
- All changes are traceable for compliance.

---

## User Management
- Go to the "User Management" page (admin only).
- Add, edit, or remove users.
- Bulk import users if needed.

---

## Reports
- Go to the "Reports" page.
- Generate and export reports on equipment, calibration, maintenance, and reservations.

---

## Accessibility & Usability
- All forms are accessible (ARIA labels, keyboard navigation).
- Input fields are validated and sanitized.
- Confirmation dialogs prevent accidental actions.

---

## Troubleshooting
- If you encounter errors, check for validation messages.
- For login issues, contact your administrator.
- For equipment or consumable issues, ensure all required fields are filled.

---

## Security & Audit
- All actions are logged for audit.
- Your email is recorded with each change.
- Only authorized users can access sensitive features.

---

## FAQ
- **Can I check out multiple items at once?**
  - Yes, select multiple items in the check-out page.
- **What happens if I forget to provide a reason for poor condition?**
  - The app will prompt you to enter a reason before proceeding.
- **How do I view audit logs?**
  - Go to the Audit Log page and use filters to find relevant entries.

---

## Support
- For help, contact your administrator or support team.
